My Detail CV.

My name is Zhenyu Yu (喻臻钰), currently a PostDoc Fellow at Guangzhou University. I received my PhD degree in Geographical Information System (GIS) from Yunnan Normal University in 2022. My research interests are wide-ranging, and my previous research mainly centered on AI for Science, GIS, and Lake Environment, including spatio-temporal analysis, time series prediction, super-resolution reconstruction, semantic segmentation, style transfer, etc.

I am currently most interested in Data Visualization and Human-AI interaction, but I am still in the process of learning, so if there is any interested partner, please don’t hesitate to contact me. Besides, I am also interested in Transfer Learning and Lifelong (Continual) Learning, and hope to have the opportunity to study and research in depth.

The world is so big, I want to see it.

After living in my hometown for nearly thirty years, coming to Guangzhou as a postdoctoral fellow is the first step for me to see the world, and I am currently looking for the next opportunity to see the world … …
